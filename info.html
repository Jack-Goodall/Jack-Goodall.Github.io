About us:

Kelham Is a place for people to learn about the past of sheffield, this is a preservation of history through means most people can experience with visual and auditory information. This museum was opened in 1982 to house any archive material that sheffield had to offer from its industrial story. kelham island offers a wealth of information such as the river don steam engine in action between 12pm and 2pm, old cars and history of WW2 such as anderson shelters.
